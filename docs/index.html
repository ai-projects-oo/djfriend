Step 1 — Load your music

Click Analyze in the top-right corner. Four ways to bring in your library:

📁  Scan a folder

Open Settings (⚙), set your Music Folder, then click Analyze → Folder. DJFriend reads BPM, key, and energy from every track.

Mac + Windows

🎵  Apple Music

Click Analyze → Apple Music and pick a playlist. Uses your existing library — no export needed.

Mac only

📄  M3U / TXT file

Click Analyze → Import M3U / TXT. Works with exports from Rekordbox, Serato, and others.

Mac + Windows

🎚️  Rekordbox XML

Export from Rekordbox: File → Export Collection in xml format. Then Analyze → Import Rekordbox XML.

Mac + Windows
Cached results: Loading takes a moment the first time. On future opens DJFriend loads your library instantly from the local cache.

Steps 2–4 — Build your set

Three quick setup steps, then hit Generate.

2

Shape your energy arc

The Energy Curve editor shows 5 draggable handles across your set timeline. Drag handles up for high-energy moments, down for low.

Use a preset to start: Build-up, Peak, Steady, Valley, or W-shape.

3

Set duration and filters

Choose how many minutes your set should run (30–180 min). Optionally open Filters to narrow by genre, vibe, mood, venue type, time of night, or vocal style.

4

Generate and refine

Click Generate. DJFriend builds a tracklist optimized for harmonic flow, BPM continuity, and your energy curve.

From here: drag rows to reorder · click ⚠ to fix a key clash · use the swap icon to browse alternatives · hit Regenerate for a different shuffle.

Step 5 — Export your set

Get your set into your DJ software or onto Spotify.

💾

Save as M3U

Click Export → Save M3U. The playlist is saved to your Playlists folder (set in Settings). Load it in Rekordbox, Serato, or any other DJ software.

Create Spotify playlist

Click Export → Create Spotify Playlist. Authorize with your Spotify account — DJFriend creates a new playlist there automatically.

Settings

Open Settings with the ⚙ icon in the top-right corner.
